**MitoNano Engine V14 — The AI System Breakdown**

MitoNano is not a general purpose conversational AI. It is a **specialised, embedded clinical grade AI system** focused on real-time adaptive simulation, decision-making, and orchestration in complex biological/clinical domains (mitochondrial, cellular, or nanomedicine modeling and treatment infrastructure).

### Core Nature of the AI

MitoNano’s AI is a **hybrid adaptive control system** that combines:
- Physics-informed simulation
- Reinforcement Learning (RL) elements
- Statistical drift detection and self-remediation
- Federated provenance and trust mechanisms
- Topology-aware and load-adaptive computation

It continuously closes the loop between **observation → modeling → decision → remediation → taxonomy evolution**.

### High-Level AI Architecture

The AI operates across three main layers:

1. **Simulation Layer (`sim/`) — The Cognitive Core**
2. **GPU / Numerical Layer (`gpu/`) — The Perceptual & Computational Substrate**
3. **Orchestration Layer (`orchestration/`) — The Executive & Risk-Management Layer**

---

### Thorough Component Breakdown

#### 1. Simulation Layer (`sim/`) — The “Brain”

This is where the primary AI intelligence lives.

**A. LoadAdaptiveResegmentingBoundaryAttenuatedModel (`morphogenesis.rs`)**  
- **Role**: Models dynamic boundary evolution in the simulated biological system (e.g., cell membrane domains, tissue compartments, or state clusters).  
- **AI Capability**: Meta-adaptation of its own update frequency. It tracks `changed_fraction` trends and shortens/lengthens resegmentation cadence accordingly.  
- **Why it matters**: Prevents wasting compute on stable regions while staying responsive during critical transitions.

**B. PromotionCapableTaxonomyExtendingNet (`rl_agent.rs`)** — The Core Learning Agent
- **Role**: This is the most explicit “RL Agent” component.  
- **Key Functions**:
  - Maintains a growing taxonomy of clinical transition types using temporal attention grouping.
  - Proposes provisional clusters for new observed patterns.
  - Uses a **promotion ledger** to track human-confirmed instances across review sessions.
  - Once thresholds are met (`confirmed_instances_required` + `min_distinct_review_sessions`), it promotes the provisional cluster into a first-class `TransitionType`.
- **AI Style**: Semi-supervised taxonomy learning with human-in-the-loop promotion. Combines unsupervised pattern discovery with RL-like credit assignment and attention mechanisms.

**C. TwoSidedBaselineRelativeClusterCorroboratedTracker (`ivis.rs`)**  
- **Role**: Multi-frame entity tracking (e.g., cells, particles, patients, or molecular states).  
- **AI Capability**: Two-sided anomaly detection on corroboration rates (both suspiciously high and suspiciously low).  
- **Clinical Value**: Detects both over-confident false positives and dangerous track fragmentation (lost or split identities).

**D. OverlapTolerantFederatedAuthorityLedger (`provenance.rs`)**  
- **Role**: Manages trust, authority, and governance over decisions in a federated setting.  
- **AI Capability**: Fuzzy-overlap recency quorum that defeats simple evasion tactics (e.g., swapping one cosigner).  
- **Why AI?**: Embodies adversarial robustness and probabilistic trust modeling.

**E. Engine & Supporting Systems**
- `engine.rs`: Orchestrates the global tick loop.
- `params.rs`: Configurable “personality” of the AI via feature flags.
- `stats.rs`: Self-monitoring and explainability layer (all adaptive decisions are traced).

---

#### 2. GPU Layer (`gpu/`) — The Perceptual & Fast Inference Engine

**A. TopologyScaledBurstDetector (`adjoint_gpu.rs`)**  
- Detects sudden “bursts” or regime shifts in the system.  
- Scales sensitivity intelligently with fleet/source size (avoids being over-sensitive in large systems).  
- Acts as an **attention mechanism** for the broader AI.

**B. StagedReversibleBoundNarrowing (`shader_verifier.rs`)**  
- Performs safe, incremental tightening of fairness/liveness bounds.  
- Each stage is independently reversible.  
- Embodies **principled, cautious constraint learning**.

---

#### 3. Orchestration Layer — The Executive Function

**LocallyRefittingParetoFrontierModel (`cost_model.rs`)**  
- Monitors drift in routing/encoding decisions.  
- Performs **bounded local refits** on individual regions using recent realized outcomes.  
- This is the AI’s **self-correction loop** for operational decisions that affect real clinical outcomes.

### Architectural Philosophy

MitoNano’s AI follows several strong axioms:
- **Compositionality**: New intelligence wraps old intelligence without breaking it.
- **Proportional Response**: Effort scales with actual drift/risk/burstiness.
- **Auditability & Reversibility**: Every adaptive step is logged and (where possible) reversible.
- **Human-Augmented**: Critical taxonomy promotions require human confirmation.
- **Defense in Depth**: Multiple overlapping safety mechanisms (two-sided checks, fuzzy quorum, staged remediation, local refits).

In essence, MitoNano V14 is a **self-improving clinical simulation & control AI** that learns new biological/clinical patterns, adapts its computational strategy, maintains trust, and corrects its own decisions — all while preserving strict safety and explainability properties required for real medical deployment.
